# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Developers and designer-developers who want a dramatic yearly story of their terminal behavior. They use this in personal reflection, portfolio sharing, and social posting contexts where visual identity matters as much as the data itself.

## Product Purpose
Turn shell history into a bold visual narrative that feels collectible and shareable. Success means users instantly understand the headline insights, want to scroll through every card, and feel the output represents their coding identity.

## Brand Personality
Bold, graphic, confident.

## Anti-references
Do not look like a standard SaaS analytics dashboard.
Do not rely on soft gradient-heavy glassmorphism.
Do not use tiny utility typography as the dominant visual voice.

## Design Principles
Lead with iconic visual cards, not utility panels.
Make each section feel poster-like and immediately legible.
Keep data narration short, clear, and emotionally charged.
Use flat, decisive color fields with strong contrast.
Maintain consistent visual grammar across all sections.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Aim for WCAG AA contrast for core text and chart labels. Respect reduced motion preferences by keeping animations subtle and non-essential. Ensure mobile and desktop layouts preserve hierarchy and readability.
