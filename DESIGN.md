# DESIGN

## Visual Direction
Flat-color editorial posters inspired by modern music storytelling interfaces.

## Color Strategy
Committed palette with solid fills only, no gradients:
- `--bg`: #050505
- `--lime`: #d8ef3f
- `--pink`: #f172cd
- `--green`: #1ed760
- `--blue`: #38bdf8
- `--ink`: #111111

## Typography
Display: Syne (heavy weights for section titles)
Body/UI: Space Grotesk
High contrast hierarchy with large titles and concise support copy.

## Component Language
Rounded story cards with flat fills and strong borders.
Micro-labels are limited and secondary.
Data blocks must inherit the same card language as hero sections.

## Motion
Short ease-out transitions only. Motion supports reveal and emphasis, never decoration.

## Layout
Content-hugging cards with tight vertical rhythm. No forced viewport-height empty space.
